⚠️ IMPORTANT: Downgrade Rules

❌ Rule 1: Watch at least 300 ads per day

If you watch LESS than 300 ads in one day, you will LOSE 1 level at midnight.

Example: You are Gold level today, but only watched 250 ads.
→ At midnight UTC, you drop to Silver level (-2000 XP)

❌ Rule 2: Stay active every day

If you don't open the app for 1 FULL DAY (24 hours), you will be RESET to Bronze level (0 XP).

Example: Last activity: Monday 10:00 AM
→ If you don't return by Tuesday 10:00 AM, you reset to Bronze!

💡 Quick Tips to Keep Your Level

✅ Watch at least 300 ads daily (150 Simple + 150 Wheel = Safe!)
✅ Open the app every day (even just for 1 minute)
✅ Check your progress in "My Level" tab
✅ Complete daily tasks for bonus XP
✅ Stay consistent - Small daily effort = Big rewards!
